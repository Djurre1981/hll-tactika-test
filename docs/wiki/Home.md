# Tactika manual

Welcome — this is the **in-app handbook** for Tactika (The Circle). Use it when you need a quick answer while preparing or reviewing a match.

Open it anytime with the **?** next to your avatar (top right).

## How this manual is organized

| Section | Use it when you want to… |
|---------|--------------------------|
| **Getting started** | Sign in, find your bearings, open the right tool |
| **How-to guides** | Complete a concrete task (RSVP, attach a strat, plan a route) |
| **Reference** | Look up roles, locks, or what each Management tab does |
| **Troubleshooting** | Fix common “why can’t I…?” problems |

> Tip: Prefer the how-to pages for match night. Skim **Roles & permissions** once so you know what your Steam role allows.
