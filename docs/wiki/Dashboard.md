# Dashboard & hub

**Goal:** use the home screen as your command center before opening tools.

## What’s on the hub

| Area | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **Next match** | Countdown, **In** count / optional seat cap, **In** / **Maybe** / **Out** |
| **Season KPIs** | Win rate, record, recent form (team pulse) |
| **My matches** | Matches where your Steam ID was on our side |
| **My tasks** | Event prep rows + legacy prep tasks assigned to you |
| **Tool launcher** | Climbing Guide, Stratmaker, Routeplanner, Micro Prep, Calendar, Records, Management (staff) |
| **Presence** | Online teammates under your avatar |

## RSVP from the hub

1. Find the next-match card.
2. While RSVP is open, choose **In**, **Maybe**, or **Out**.
3. After staff **Close RSVP**, confirmed **In** players can still **Raincheck** with a reason.
4. Change your mind while RSVP is open from the same card or the Match Brief.

Editors/admins create and edit events on **Calendar**; members mainly RSVP and open linked prep.
