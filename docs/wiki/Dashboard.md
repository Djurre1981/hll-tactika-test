# Dashboard & hub

**Goal:** use the home screen as your command center before opening tools.

## What’s on the hub

| Area | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **Next match** | Countdown, seats, **I’m in** / **Raincheck** |
| **Season KPIs** | Win rate, record, recent form (team pulse) |
| **My matches** | Matches where your Steam ID was on our side |
| **My tasks** | Prep tasks assigned to you across events |
| **Tool launcher** | Climbing Guide, Stratmaker, Routeplanner, Micro Prep, Calendar, Records, Management (staff) |
| **Presence** | Online teammates under your avatar |

![Hub layout](placeholder)

## RSVP from the hub

1. Find the next-match card.
2. Choose **I’m in** or **Raincheck** (raincheck asks for a short reason).
3. Change your mind later from the same card or the Match Brief.

Editors/admins create and edit events on **Calendar**; members mainly RSVP and open linked prep.

## Presence & account

- Hover your **avatar** (top right) for **Admin Panel** (if allowed) and **Sign out**.
- Click **?** left of the avatar to open this manual.
