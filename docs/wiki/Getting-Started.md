# Getting started

**Goal:** get from Steam sign-in to the tool you need for tonight’s match.

## 1. Sign in

1. Open Tactika and choose **Sign in with Steam**.
2. You must be on the team allowlist. If you see **forbidden**, ask an Owner to add your Steam ID under **Admin Panel → Team**.

## 2. Find the hub

After login you land on the **Dashboard** (home). From here you can:

- See the **next match** and RSVP (**In** / **Maybe** / **Out**)
- Open **Calendar**, **Records**, and tools (Climbing Guide, Stratmaker, Routeplanner, Micro Prep)
- Check **My matches** and **My tasks**
- See who’s online (bubbles under your avatar)

![Where to look on the hub](placeholder)

*Top right: avatar menu (Admin / Sign out) and **?** help. Center: widgets and tool tiles.*

## 3. Match-night path (recommended)

1. Open the upcoming event from the hub or **Calendar**.
2. On **Match Brief**, confirm map / faction / team and linked prep; **RSVP** (**In** / **Maybe** / **Out**).
3. Open attached **Strat**, **Route plan**, **Micro Prep**, or **LineUp** from the brief chips.
4. Complete your **event prep** rows when done. Comp Members can view LineUp; Admins fill squads from the **In** pool.

## 4. Know your role

Site access is role-based (Comp Member → Owner). Read [Roles & permissions](Roles-and-Permissions) once. Comp Members can browse and RSVP; creating/editing content needs Comp Advisor or higher.

## 5. Quick UI tip — searchable dropdowns

Tactika menus use glass dropdowns. When a list is open, **start typing** to filter options (player names, maps, etc.). Use **↑ / ↓** and **Enter** to pick; **Esc** closes the menu.
